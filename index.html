WHY WE EXIST

Before starting BNDR LLC, I spent 20 years in retail sales, sales management, and training across many different industries. I fell in love and studied the psychology of sales and worked directly with small business owners as well as upper management. I've seen exactly how successful businesses operate and why others fail, and I bring that focus on client satisfaction directly into my builds.

I work deep in AI every single day. The landscape shifts constantly, the training of core models changes dramatically from one update, that means every word that you write when prompting can instantly have an impact on the results you receive, and I track those nuances closely. If you have integrated AI, or are considering it, but are concerned about its viability, or that what you already have is not being maximized or used properly at all, I can help you navigate that. I specialize in complex system prompting to make that transition effortless for you and highly effective.

Our rates won't crush you. I'm building my business entirely on referrals, which means I treat every single project as if my next job depends on your recommendation.

If you have questions or want to discuss a project, please reach out.
